Independent Survey Management: This survey is being managed independently by Pure Survey (www.puresurvey.co.za), a professional survey platform specializing in confidential employee feedback. This ensures complete independence and neutrality in data collection and analysis.

Complete Confidentiality: Your individual responses are completely confidential and anonymous. No one at Sanlam, including management, HR, or any other department, will have access to your individual responses.
